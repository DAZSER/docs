Cost of Living Adjustments

To upload a Cost of Living Adjustment file, upload a new CSV file with the column titles of id,lastColaAmount,lastColaDate,newAmount to the S3 Bucket s3://dazser-files/cola/{{ regionName }}/*.csv. This will kick off backend/cola.ts which will process the CSV file and upload the results to specified region.

regionName Region
baltimore Baltimore
birmingham Birmingham
orlando Orlando
tampa Tampa

Example:

s3://dazser-files/cola/tampa/colafor2022.csv
---
id,newAmount,lastColaDate,lastColaAmount
4517,1168,10/1/2021,1
7497,1,10/1/2021,1
